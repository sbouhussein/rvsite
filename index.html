Our mission

RandezVous was founded to help provide people with quick and easy access to personalized and sustainable activities that positively affect their well-being. The Randez-Vous App aims to provide a platform where users can input their interests and location, among other things, and be instantly provided with a fun activity to do alone or with friends. This app aspires to help people globally, find more fulfilling and impactful ways to spend their free time
